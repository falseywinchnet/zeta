# Transformer sense-bridge correspondence

- Author: Repository contributor
- Date: 2026-07-12
- Status: supplied conceptual proposal

Transformer representations place words and terms in a learned perceptual
projection. Exposure lets the model represent smooth differences among many
concepts, although the geometry can be jagged. A transformer can therefore act
as an efficient search engine when it has been trained for equivalence.

The immediate proposal is a small model or engine that recognizes word and term
equivalence and assigns synonymous expressions a common fixed embedding address.
It would serve as a translation layer between surface language and the semantic
identity or interpretation graph. Its initial reach may be lexical rather than
full-statement equivalence.
