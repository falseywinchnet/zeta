# Apparent obstruction resolved

The independent product coupling of the two triangular measures is not
pointwise ordered.  Its raw fiber

`integral_u^v C4/(Q^6 kappa^2)`

is negative when `u>v`, which can occur in the left-left component.  Treating
that fiber as a pointwise positivity problem would create another false
regional decomposition.

The exact CDF crossing kernel is `W=F_mu-F_nu`.  Its positivity follows from
one density-ratio crossing, so the negative independent-coupling fibers cancel
analytically.  A monotone coupling removes them altogether.

No nonzero transport remainder was found: equation (8) of
`TRANSPORT_KERNEL.md` cancels it identically.
