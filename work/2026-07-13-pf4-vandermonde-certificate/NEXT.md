# Next proof obligations

1. Produce division-free, common-subexpression formulas for the theta
   derivatives and curvature jets needed by the integral means.
2. Certify those one-variable jets by centered Taylor remainder bounds on a
   finite compact partition; avoid direct broad-ball cumulant evaluation.
3. Implement dependency-aware Taylor arithmetic for `Jhat(m,rho,theta)` and
   verify it against the exact collision and exponential-model identities.
4. Prove and encode the boundary modules `rho=0`, `theta=0`, `theta=1`, and the
   reflection boundary.
5. Prove analytic tail and large-separation lemmas that reduce the unbounded
   domain to a compact core.
6. Cover that core with directed Taylor cells and emit a replayable manifest
   containing domains, precision, formulas, dependencies, commands, and output
   hashes.

Only the completion of all six obligations would support promotion of the
three-point inequality.  The exact-decimal PF5 witness audit can be integrated
separately in a later refine round.
