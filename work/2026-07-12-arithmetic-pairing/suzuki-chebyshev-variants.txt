                                                       ON VARIANTS OF CHEBYSHEV’S CONJECTURE

                                                                                   MASATOSHI SUZUKI


                                                   Abstract. We show that the sign constancy for the values of certain weighted sum-
                                                   matory functions of the von Mangoldt function implies the Riemann hypothesis or the
                                                   generalized Riemann hypothesis for Dirichlet L-functions. While such sign constancy
                                                   is challenging to establish individually, we prove that the summatory functions under
                                                   study have constant signs on average.
