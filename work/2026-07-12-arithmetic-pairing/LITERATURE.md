# Literature findings

## Suzuki 2012

*On monotonicity of certain weighted summatory functions associated with
L-functions* proves that the kernel has one zero and that eventual nonnegative
sign of the weighted sum for all `0<omega<1/2` is equivalent to RH. The paper
explicitly notes that proving such monotonicity is hard and does not reduce it
to a separate established problem.

## Suzuki 2025

*On variants of Chebyshev's conjecture* studies related sign-constant weighted
summatory functions. It proves constant sign after averaging in settings where
individual sign constancy remains conditional. This does not imply the
pointwise dominance required here.

The literature therefore removes two false shortcuts:

- the kernel's one-crossing property is already known;
- averaged sign is not the required pointwise sign theorem.
