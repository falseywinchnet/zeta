# Quantitative edge collision and separated complement

Let `h=r-m` and `X=pi exp(2m)`.  Substitute the dependency-preserving fourth
order forms

\[
A=hu_0+{h^2u_1\over2}+{h^3u_2\over6}+h^4a_3,
\]

\[
q^{(j)}(m+h)=u_j+hu_{j+1}+{h^2u_{j+2}\over2}
 +{h^3u_{j+3}\over6}+h^4g_j.
\]

The exact cleared numerator `N_S` of `S_r` then satisfies

\[
N_S=h^4\mathcal N(h).
\]

At collision,

\[
\mathcal N(0)={qC_4\over6}>{44392\over6}X^7.
\]

On `0<=h<=1/4`, `X(m+t)<2X`; the certified tail jets imply rational boxes for
all corrected endpoint remainder variables.  Exact sparse coefficient
summation gives

\[
|\mathcal N'(h)|\le
{9691662950748701720576\over3875090625}X^7.
\]

The square-root denominator factor also divides by `h^2`; its collision value
is `-C3/2`, and

\[
|\mathcal G'(h)|\le {3595695104\over18225}X^3.
\]

Therefore the rational choice

\[
\boxed{h_0={7167625959375\over4845831475374350860288}}
\]

keeps the numerator positive and the denominator nonzero.  Hence `S_r>0` for
`0<h<=h0` and `X>=23`.

For `h>=h0`,

\[
V-1=e^{2h}-1\ge2h>2^{-29}.
\]

\[
|\Delta q^{(j)}(r)-\Delta q^{(j)}(m)|
\le {K_{j+3}\over8X^4}(1-V^{-4}),
\]

\[

\]

the numerator of the full-theta comparison

\[
S_r-\frac{\lambda_S}{2}q_1(r)

\[
\boxed{S_r(m,r)>0\quad(X_m\ge23,\ m<r).}

density and not global PF4.
