# Rejected form

Dividing jet coordinate `j` by `x^j` gives useful uniform coordinate bounds,
but a constant perturbation box then loses the exponential dependence while
the scaled two-mode cleared values approach zero.  That form cannot prove a
uniform sign on the entire half-line.

This failure is purely an abstraction failure.  The unscaled perturbation
retains `x^w exp(-8*k*x)` and is uniformly dominated by its value at `x=5`.
No additional analytic assumption is required.
