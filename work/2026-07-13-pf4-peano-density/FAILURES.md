# Insufficient or rejected steps

## Direct convexity of E

The identity `E(m,r)=integral (r-z)E_rr(m,z) dz` is correct, and `E_rr` was
positive at the named diagnostics.  But its endpoint numerator has 41 terms
and hides the positive first derivative.  The sharper factorization
`E_r=P R S` reduces the proof to the 24-term `S_r` numerator and is preferred.

## Collision positivity alone

The generic expansion proves a positive leading term in every collision
direction, but without a uniform remainder it proves only a local asymptotic
statement at each base point.  It does not establish a global neighborhood of
the diagonal.

## Diagnostic monotonicity values

Positive values of `S_r` and `J_b` at twelve named configurations do not prove
their global signs.  They justify investing in the algebraic densities; they
are sealed from the proof chain.

## Unstructured tail substitution

Substituting raw theta bounds directly into the 24- and 74-term numerators
would lose gap cancellation near `U=V=1`.  The proof must separate resolved
gaps from the collision regime and retain the exact `C4` leading term.
