# PO-0023 — triangular integral formula for Lambda

Frame: S06

Statement: for `p<z<w`, let `Q1=Q'`, `Q2=Q1'`, let the continuous function
`kappa=2-Q2`, and define

\[
\Lambda=(w-p)+Q[p,z]-Q[z,w].
\]

Then

\[
\Lambda=\frac1{z-p}\int_p^z(t-p)\kappa(t)\,dt+
\frac1{w-z}\int_z^w(w-t)\kappa(t)\,dt.
\]

Proof: `PF4.Curvature.increasing_integral` and `decreasing_integral` exhibit
explicit primitives derived from the actual derivative jets; continuity
supplies their FTC integrability premises. Substitution and clearing the two
proved-nonzero interval lengths gives
`coordinateLambda_eq_triangular`.

Non-vacuity witness: `Q(t)=t`, `Q1(t)=1`, `Q2(t)=0` and
`p=0,z=1,w=2` satisfy the derivative and ordering hypotheses.

Counterexample condition: a twice-differentiable `Q` and ordered triple for
which the coordinate endpoint expression differs from the two displayed
integrals.

Formalization: `PF4.Curvature.coordinateLambda_eq_triangular`.

Status: `FORMALLY_PROVED`.
