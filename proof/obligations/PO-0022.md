# PO-0022 — positive actual coordinate rho and kappa

Frame: S06

Statement: on the complete actual coordinate range,
`rho=1-Q''=F2/q^3>0` and `kappa=2-Q''=1+rho>1`.

Formalization:
`PF4.PaperObjectClosure.actualCoordinateRho_apply_kernelCoordinate` proves the
exact quotient identity, and `actualCoordinateRhoKappa_pos_on_range` packages
both strict signs and `kappa=1+rho` for every point in the actual range.

The theorem does not claim that the coordinate map is onto `R` and uses no
property of the canonical inverse extension outside its range.

Counterexample condition: an actual coordinate point where the quotient
identity fails, `rho<=0`, or `kappa<=1`.
