# PO-0040 — strict positivity of the transport numerator

Frame: S09–S10

Statement:

\[
N=\delta\Lambda\int_p^w
\Delta(t)\frac{C_4(t)}{Q(t)^6\kappa(t)^2}\,dt>0.
\]

Formalization: `PF4.Transport.coordinateTransportNumerator_pos_closed` uses
the actual coordinate definitions of `δ`, `Λ`, and the closed
`PF4.Cumulative.coordinateGap`. It derives both normalization identities,
branch matching and continuity at `z`, positivity on the open interval,
continuity and positivity of the displayed curvature weight, and strict
positivity of `N`. No measure or CDF premise occurs.

`PF4.Transport.concrete_transportNumerator_pos_from_measures` remains an
independent measure/CDF validation route.

Counterexample condition: an admissible interior point with nonpositive gap or
weight, zero interval length, or a nonpositive value of the displayed
numerator.

Status: `FORMAL_FRAGMENT`. The strict integral mechanism is checked from
explicit `Q`, curvature, and `C₄` sign premises; their actual Riemann-kernel
instances remain upstream.
