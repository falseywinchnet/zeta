# PO-0038 — transport expectation object identity

Frame: S08

Statement: for the concrete probability measures `μ,ν` and the paper's
primitive `A₀`, the algebraic transport quantity satisfies

\[
K=\mathbb E_\nu[A_0]-\mathbb E_\mu[A_0].
\]

Required proof: define `A₀` and both Bochner integrals in Lean, prove
integrability from compact support and continuity, then identify the expanded
endpoint formula with the two expectations. Equality of sampled or symbolic
stand-ins is not sufficient.

Counterexample condition: a permitted parameter choice for which the expanded
`K` differs from either concrete expectation difference or one expectation is
undefined.

Status: `SYMBOLICALLY_CHECKED`; Lean object identity open.
