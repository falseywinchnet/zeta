# PO-0033 — identity of the actual density ratio

Frame: S09

Statement: Under PO-0030 and PO-0031, for every `t` with `p<t<z`, both
densities are strictly positive and

\[
\frac{f_\mu(t)}{f_\nu(t)}
=\frac{\Lambda(z-t)}{L\delta(t-p)}.
\]

Proof: On `(p,z)` the active pieces are

\[
f_\mu(t)=\frac{(z-t)\kappa(t)}{L^2\delta},\qquad
f_\nu(t)=\frac{(t-p)\kappa(t)}{L\Lambda}.
\]

All factors `z-t`, `t-p`, `κ(t)`, `L`, `δ`, and `Λ` are strictly positive, so
division and cancellation of `κ(t)` are legitimate. Exact fraction
simplification gives the statement.

Object identity: these are the densities already proved to normalize the
actual measures, not fresh symbolic expressions.

Counterexample condition: a point in `(p,z)` where a denominator vanishes or
the actual ratio differs from the displayed function.

Formalization: the density object identity and every denominator sign are
checked by `PF4.Densities.leftDensity_ratio`.

Status: `DEPRECATED_OPTIONAL`. Retained as an independent density identity;
the deterministic closed-gap proof bypasses the ratio route.
