# PO-0028 — determinant-to-curvature identity

Frame: S07

Statement: `C4 = Q^6 kappa^2 D`.

Formalization: `PF4.C4Invariant.determinantC4` is the determinant of the
primary `4 x 4` central-moment Hankel matrix.
`determinantC4_eq_cumulantC4Polynomial` derives its thirteen-term expansion,
the coordinate derivative tower derives the five substituted cumulants, and
`coordinateDeterminantC4_eq_derivedC4` proves the exact curvature
factorization after discharging the curvature denominator.

Non-vacuity: `C4` is not defined as the desired curvature numerator, and no
positivity or numerical evaluation proves the equality.

Counterexample condition: a real coordinate jet with nonzero curvature for
which the primary central-moment determinant differs from the cleared
primitive-derived curvature numerator.

Status: `FORMALLY_PROVED`.
