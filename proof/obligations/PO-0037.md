# PO-0037 — strict cumulative gap

Frame: S09

Statement: For the coordinate-derived normalized triangular weights, let
`Δ` be the piecewise endpoint function `PF4.Cumulative.coordinateGap`. Then

\[
\Delta(p)=\Delta(w)=0,
\qquad
\Delta(t)>0\quad(p<t<w).
\]

The actual measure-backed CDF difference of PO-0030 and PO-0031 is an
independently checked interface equal to this function.

Proof:

1. Both measures are supported in `[p,w]`, are absolutely continuous (hence
   have no endpoint atoms), and have mass one. For the standard CDF
   `F(t)=μ((−∞,t])`, both CDFs are zero at `p` and one at `w`; therefore the gap
   is zero at both endpoints.
2. For `p<t≤t_*`, PO-0036 gives a strictly positive density difference on the
   nonempty interval `(p,t)`, so
   `Δ(t)=∫_p^t(fμ-fν)>0`.
3. PO-0032 gives

   \[
   \Delta(z)=1-F_\nu(z)=\nu((z,w])>0.
   \]

4. For `t_*≤t<z`, PO-0036 gives `Δ'<0`; hence

   \[
   \Delta(t)=\Delta(z)-\int_t^z\Delta'(u)du>\Delta(z)>0.
   \]

5. For `z≤t<w`, `Fμ(t)=1` and

   \[
   \Delta(t)=1-F_\nu(t)=\nu((t,w])>0,
   \]

   because the right density is strictly positive on `(t,w)`.

The intervals in Steps 2, 4, and 5 cover `(p,w)`. The proof uses strict right
mass from the actual density; it never introduces an assumed-positive endpoint
symbol.

Counterexample condition: an interior `t` with `Δ(t)≤0`, or a nonzero endpoint
gap.

Formalization: `PF4.Cumulative` defines the proof-facing gap without measures
and proves its two explicit endpoint forms and endpoint zeros.
`coordinateGap_pos_of_normalized` proves strict positivity from the explicit
density crossing and exact normalized integrals; after the crossing it exposes
the positive right-tail integral plus a nonnegative reverse-density integral.
`coordinateGap_pos` derives the actual coordinate normalizers and both mass
identities internally. `coordinateGap_continuous_of_normalized` proves exact
left/right branch matching at `z`.
`PF4.CDF.cdfGap_eq_closedGapLeft` and `cdfGap_eq_closedGapRight` identify the
legacy measure-backed gap with those forms. `PF4.CDF.cdfGap_pos` separately
checks strict positivity in the three regions as an independent validation
interface.

Status: `FORMALLY_PROVED`.
