\paragraph{Code and data availability.}

The public repository is
\url{https://github.com/falseywinchnet/zeta}. The exact analytic proof was
promoted in commit
\href{https://github.com/falseywinchnet/zeta/commit/0ebb95b141a109f88dc0c0218a9840e01eba9790}{\texttt{0ebb95b141a1}}.
The submission source, generated coefficient table, environment lock, replay
tool, and PDF are frozen together under the versioned tag
\texttt{pf4-paper-v1.0.1}; their SHA-256 values are listed in
\path{paper/RELEASE_MANIFEST.md}. The complete evidence replay is
\texttt{python scripts/replay\_paper.py} from the repository root.

All theorem-bearing sign decisions are exact. Numerical values produced by
the repository scripts are diagnostic only; there is no separate experimental
dataset.
